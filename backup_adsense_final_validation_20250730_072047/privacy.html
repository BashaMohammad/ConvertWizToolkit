Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect your data.

Last updated: January 2025

What This Means for You

ConvertWiz is designed with your privacy in mind. All file conversions and processing happen directly in your web browser, which means your files never leave your device or get uploaded to our servers.

Your files stay private and secure on your own device

Information We Collect

Files and Content

We do not collect, store, or have access to any files you upload or convert. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.

Usage Statistics

We may collect basic website usage information (like page visits) to improve our service, but this does not include any personal files or conversion data.

Technical Information

Standard web information like your browser type and IP address may be logged for security and performance purposes, similar to any website you visit.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our conversion tools
  • To ensure website security and prevent abuse
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties

Data Security

Since all file processing happens in your browser, your files never travel over the internet to our servers. This client-side approach provides the highest level of privacy and security for your documents and images.

Your browser acts as your personal, secure conversion workspace

Cookies and Local Storage

We may use browser local storage to remember your preferences (like daily usage limits for certain tools) and improve your experience. This information stays on your device and is not shared with us.

Third-Party Services

Some tools may connect to external services (like currency exchange rates or IP lookup services) to provide current information. These connections are made directly from your browser and follow the privacy policies of those services.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data since it never leaves your device. You can:

  • • Use all tools without creating an account
  • • Clear your browser data at any time
  • • Stop using the service at any time
  • • Contact us with any privacy questions

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display ads on ConvertWiz. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies (such as the DoubleClick DART cookie) enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to ConvertWiz and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt-out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can visit www.aboutads.info to learn how to opt-out of third-party vendors' use of cookies.

We do not have control over cookies used by third-party advertisers. Please refer to Google's Advertising Policies for more details.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle data, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're committed to being transparent about our practices.
