Privacy & Security

100% Client-Side Processing - Your files never leave your device

Absolutely. All file conversions happen directly in your browser using client-side processing. We never upload, store, or access your files on our servers. Your data remains completely private and secure on your device throughout the entire conversion process.

This architecture ensures maximum privacy protection. Your files never leave your device, eliminating concerns about data breaches, unauthorized access, or compliance violations.

ConvertWiz collects minimal information necessary for website functionality and improvement. We use standard web analytics to understand usage patterns and optimize performance, but we do not collect personal identifiers, store conversion history, or track individual user behavior.
